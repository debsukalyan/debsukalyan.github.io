About Me

I have grown up all around the country owing to my father's job in the Army. One thing that always fascinated me was human behaviour. Currently, I am a cognitive neuroscience researcher working at the intersection of emotion, cognition, and motivation. In my research I design behavioural tasks, collect data, and analyse both behaviour and brain activity (fMRI) to understand how the mind regulates emotional conflict.

An allied interest of mine is applying behavioural science to solve real-world problems — especially in marketing and consumer behaviour.
