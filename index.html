About Me

I have grown up all around the country owing to my father's job in the Army. One thing that always fascinated me was human behaviour. Currently, I am a cognitive neuroscience researcher working at the intersection of emotion, cognition, and motivation. As part of my research in the Emotion and Cognition Lab, IISc Bangalore, I design behavioural tasks, collect data, and analyse both behaviour and brain activity (fMRI) to understand cognitive control of emotional conflict.

An allied interest of mine is applying behavioural science to solve real-world problems — especially in marketing and consumer behaviour.

Discover how I combine rigorous experiments with real-world curiosity.
