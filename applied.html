Applied Behavioural Science

Beyond the lab, I seek to translate cognitive and behavioural science insights to real‑world contexts. I collaborate with organisations to understand human decision‑making, design engaging experiences, and derive actionable insights.

  • 1001 Stories (India) — Remote intern, Jul–Aug 2024: Conducted 45 cross‑cultural interviews on perceptions of “premium” and mapped customer journeys to build five personas for e‑ and quick‑commerce.
  • Hive Science.ai (USA) — Remote consultant, Aug–Nov 2024: Developed Psychopy visual‑search and implicit association test tasks to measure ad effectiveness; built pandas pipelines and interactive dashboards to inform AI model training.
